Project Scope
=============

Yoobee College offers a range of courses for students. Each course consists of specific subjects, and lecturers are assigned to teach these subjects by the college.

The proposed system will manage student records, courses, subjects and lecturer information. Each entity will include its own attributes.

Entites :
	Student : Student_ID, Student_Name, Contact_No, Student_Email, Course_ID
	Course  : Course_ID, Course_Name, Course_Details
	Subjects : Subject_ID, Subject_Name, Lecturer_ID, Course_ID
	Lecturer : Lecturer_ID, Lecturer_Name, Lecturer_Email
